  Community Guidelines

Please Read and Agree to the following before making a post:

MusicKicks aims to build an inclusive and safe environment for the community while still maintaining it's purpose.

Please follow the guidelines below:

  1. No hate speech or any kinds of discrimination.
    - Comments or reviews containing or promoting hateful speech is strictly prohibited on this site.
  2. Be respectful
    - Users should engage and communicate in a respectful manner at all times.
  3. Privacy
    - Avoid sharing sensitive and personal information about yourself or others.
  4. Content relevance
    - Posts, reviews, or discussions should stay relevant to the content's topic.
  5. No spam
    - Avoid spamming, unrelated topics, self promotions or any kind of unrelated content.
  6. No illegal activities.
    - promoting or discussing illegal activities are prohibited within the site.

Any violations of these rules may lead to account suspension or a permanent ban based on the severity of the violation.
