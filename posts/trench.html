  Released in 2018, the album explores themes of mental health, personal struggles, and hope. The duo combines alternative rock, pop, and hip-hop elements to navigate the complexities of internal struggles we all face. With "Trench," Twenty One Pilots delivers a thought-provoking and innovative record, marking their return after a year-long hiatus.

Notable Songs:

Legend

  This song was written for Tyler Joseph's grandfather, Robert Joseph, who passed away on March 17, 2018. When I first heard it, it hit close to home as it reminded me of my grandmother, who also passed away years after the album's release. However, it has also helped me to remember her fondly.

Chlorine

  It is one of the most unique songs I’ve heard from the band's creations. It delivers a sense of serenity while also conveying a heavy feeling, especially since the song addresses the idea of drinking chlorine, literally or figuratively. Overall, it has a catchy tune despite the serious message it carries.

My blood

  The first song on the album tells a fictional story and explores various themes, each with its own meaning. Aside from its message, the bridge leading up to the chorus is astounding; the riffs are amazing to hear. The song also transitions smoothly into the album's second track, "Levitate."
